Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 25.9.2025

We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use n8n Upload Automation (Social Upload Automation).

1. Information We Collect

  • TikTok OAuth Data: When you connect your TikTok account, we receive an access token from TikTok. This token is stored securely in your n8n instance and is used solely to upload videos on your behalf.
  • Media Files: The Service processes media files you choose to upload. These files are transmitted to TikTok and are not stored permanently by us.

2. How We Use Information

  • To authenticate with TikTok on your behalf.
  • To upload videos to TikTok as instructed by your workflow.

3. How We Do Not Use Information

  • We do not sell or share your information with third parties.
  • We do not use your TikTok credentials for any purpose other than uploading content.

4. Data Retention

  • OAuth tokens are stored securely within your n8n environment.
  • Media files are processed transiently and are not kept beyond the execution of your automation.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to secure your tokens and data within the automation system.
